About Us

Welcome to MyCCAngel, a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting individuals living with Inflammatory Bowel Disease (IBD). Our mission is to provide a comprehensive online platform that empowers patients to manage their condition effectively, connects them with a supportive community, and offers access to valuable resources.

Our goal is to bridge the gap in IBD care by providing:

  • A safe space for community building through chat rooms, webinars, and group discussions
  • A live assistant AI chatbot for answers to IBD-related questions and dietary guidance
  • A toilet location map for easy access to nearby facilities
  • A resources library featuring articles, videos, podcasts, and updates on IBD management

We aim to facilitate early diagnosis, timely medical appointments, and affordable treatment options. Our platform is designed to be a one-stop shop for IBD patients, providing them with the tools and support they need to navigate their journey.

At MyCCAngel, we believe that no one should face IBD alone. Join our community today and become part of a supportive network that understands your needs and is dedicated to helping you thrive.

Thank you for visiting MyCCAngel. Together, let's make a difference in the lives of IBD patients worldwide!
